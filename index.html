Training And Placement Office Website of M.B.M Engineering College, Jodhpur

Backend Developer

The website maintains four types of users (Student, Company, Faculty and Alumni) along with admins. It aims at connecting all the other three types of users with the currently studying students. Students can fill up their resume builder form, see the list of upcoming companies, apply for them as per their eligibility, see the list and details of other students, professors and the latest news in their panel. Company, on registering for the placement drive in the college, gets to fill a Job Announcement Form, defining it’s criteria for recruitment. Also, the list and details of alumni registered with the website.
